About us

Trains across Europe,
made simple.

WoW Train is an independent travel platform helping travelers discover, compare and book train journeys across 9 European countries — from daily commutes to once-in-a-lifetime scenic routes.

Our mission

Train travel is the most beautiful, sustainable and underrated way to explore Europe. But booking a ticket between countries is often confusing, expensive, and buried in operator websites.

We built WoW Train to fix that — one place to see live departures, compare scenic routes, book through trusted partners, and even translate station signs in Japanese, Chinese or Korean. Everything a real European traveler actually needs.

What we offer

  • Real-time departures — live schedules from official railway APIs across 9 countries, updated every 90 seconds.
  • Scenic experiences — 13 hand-picked iconic routes: Glacier Express, Bernina Express, Cinque Terre, Rhine Valley and more.
  • Travel essentials — city passes, airport transfers, eSIMs, attraction tickets, luggage storage and travel insurance — all from verified partners.
  • Offline access — full timetables stored locally so the app works even without internet.
  • Sign translator — point your camera at any station sign (Japanese, Chinese, Korean, etc.) for instant translation.

Affiliate disclosure

WoW Train participates in affiliate programs. When you book through our partner links, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. This is how we keep the app free, ad-light and independent. We only recommend services we believe genuinely help travelers — never paid placements that don't add value.

Built independently

WoW Train is a small, independent project — not backed by any railway operator or large travel corporation. We build for travelers, not for ad networks.
