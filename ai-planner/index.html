How it works

Three steps to your perfect train trip

No forms, no dropdowns, no juggling multiple booking sites. Just describe what you want.

1

Describe your trip

Type your trip in plain language — cities, dates, how many days, must-see stops. The AI understands natural requests like "I have 10 days and want to see the Alps."

2

Get your itinerary

The AI builds a complete route with the best train connections, journey times, operators (TGV, Eurostar, AVE, Frecciarossa) and notes on scenic value and timing.

3

Book on Klook

Each leg links directly to Klook where you can compare prices and book your tickets. No extra accounts needed — just click and book.
