    Austria · Hidden gem
    Hallstatt
    Train to Hallstatt + lake ferry
    Best: May–Sep · ~3h 30 from Salzburg
    A fairytale village on a glassy alpine lake. The train drops you on the far shore and a little ferry glides you across — arriving by car never feels this magical.
    ▶ Video + guide
    Slovenia · Hidden gem
    Lake Bled
    Via Ljubljana · Bohinj Railway
    Best: Jun–Sep · island church & castle
    An island church on an emerald lake under a clifftop castle. Two stations, one secret — pick the right one and the train drops you by the water.
    ▶ Video + guide
    Switzerland · Hidden gem
    Lauterbrunnen
    Via Interlaken · Berner Oberland Bahn
    Best: Jun–Oct · 72 waterfalls flowing
    A valley of 72 waterfalls beneath sheer cliffs. No direct line — change at Interlaken onto a mountain railway that's half the magic.
    ▶ Video + guide
    France · Hidden gem
    Colmar
    Direct TGV from Paris (~2h20)
    Best: Dec markets & spring flowers
    The "Little Venice" of Alsace — half-timbered houses and flower-lined canals, a short walk from a direct TGV station.
    ▶ Video + guide
    Germany → Italy
    Munich → Venice
    EuroCity over the Brenner Pass
    ~7h · Alps, Tyrol & the Adige valley
    One of Europe's most scenic rides — from Bavaria over the Alps, down through vineyards to the edge of the Venetian lagoon.
    ▶ Video + guide

    Crossing the Alps · France to Piedmont
    From Lyon's riverside to baroque Turin, threading the Maurienne valley and the historic Alpine tunnel into Italy.

    What is WoW Train? ▾
    An independent and free platform to design scenic train itineraries across Europe. No account, no subscription — just explore, plan and book through certified partners.
    How do I book a ticket? ▾
    We connect you directly and transparently to official operators like Trainline. Search your route, click "Check schedules →" and complete the booking on the partner's secure platform.
    How do I share my experience? ▾
    Use the interactive review form: write your WoW moment (max 140 characters), choose a star rating and hit Publish. Your review goes live in the cloud in real time.
    Do you charge any fees? ▾
    100% free for the user. WoW Train receives support from official partners without any extra cost to you. Transparent, independent and always on your side.
    Is my data private? ▾
    WoW Train does not collect, store or share personal data on external servers. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. Only anonymous analytics via Google Analytics to improve the experience.
