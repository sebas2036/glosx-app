Frequently asked questions

need to know.

Is WoW Train free to use? ▾
Yes, completely free. No subscription, no paywall, no account required. Just open and search.
Which countries are supported? ▾
Spain, France, Germany, Switzerland, Italy, Belgium, Netherlands, Austria and Portugal — with real-time GTFS data from official railway operators.
How do I book a ticket? ▾
Search your route and click "Search →". You'll be redirected to Trainline — a certified operator that handles the booking securely. WoW Train never processes payments.
Does it work offline? ▾
Yes. Full timetable access without internet. GTFS data is stored locally on your device — useful when travelling through tunnels or areas with no signal.
Is my data private? ▾
WoW Train does not collect, store or share personal data on external servers. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. Only anonymous analytics via Google Analytics to improve the experience.
