Milan to Florence by Train

By WoW Train · Updated July 2026 · 4 min read

A quick high-speed hop between Italy's fashion capital and the birthplace of the Renaissance.

The journey, stop by stop

From Milano Centrale, trains head toward Florence, arriving at Firenze S.M. Novella.

How long does it take?

The trip takes around 1h 40m. Check the live schedule for your date.

Who runs the route?

The route is run by Frecciarossa and Italo, competing head to head with frequent departures all day.

Milan to Florence train price (2026)

Advance fares start around €20-30, more on the day.

How to get the best fare

  • Book early. The cheapest saver fares sell out first — booking ahead can be dramatically cheaper than buying on the day.
  • Travel off-peak. Mid-morning and mid-week departures tend to be quieter and cheaper.
