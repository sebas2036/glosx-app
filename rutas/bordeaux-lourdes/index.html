Bordeaux to Lourdes by Train

By WoW Train · Updated July 2026 · 4 min read

An alternative gateway to Lourdes from the southwest, running from the wine capital of Bordeaux down through Gascony to the Pyrenees.

The journey, stop by stop

From Bordeaux Saint-Jean, SNCF Intercités trains run south through Gascony to Lourdes, at the edge of the Pyrenees.

How long does it take?

The trip takes around 2h - 2h 30m, with several daily departures. Check the live schedule for your date.

Who runs the route?

The route is run by SNCF Intercités, with direct daily departures connecting onward to Paris and the rest of the TGV network.

Bordeaux to Lourdes train price (2026)

Fares run from around €25-40, cheaper booked in advance.

How to get the best fare

  • Book early. The cheapest saver fares sell out first — booking ahead can be dramatically cheaper than buying on the day.
  • Travel off-peak. Mid-morning and mid-week departures tend to be quieter and cheaper.
