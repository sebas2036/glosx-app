Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly, remember your preferences and understand how visitors use the service.

2. How we use cookies

WoW Train (glosx.app) uses a minimal set of cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Types of cookies we use

TypePurposeDuration
Essential Required for basic site functionality (language preference, search history). Session / 30 days
Analytics Google Analytics — anonymous usage statistics to improve the site. Up to 24 months
Affiliate tracking Identify which partner referred a sale (Travelpayouts, Awin). No personal data shared. Up to 30 days

4. Third-party services

We embed scripts from the following providers, who may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics — site traffic measurement (anonymized IP).
  • Travelpayouts — affiliate click tracking for booking partners.
  • Awin — affiliate click tracking for Trainline integrations.

These services act as independent data controllers under their own privacy policies.

5. Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View, accept or reject cookies on a per-site basis
  • Delete all cookies stored on your device
  • Block third-party cookies entirely

Disabling essential cookies may break parts of the site. Disabling analytics or affiliate cookies will not affect your ability to use the service.

6. GDPR and your rights

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access, correct or delete any personal data we hold, as described in our Privacy Policy.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision.
